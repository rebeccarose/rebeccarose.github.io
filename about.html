About me

Research is the secret sauce to making products and services that people love.

My designs and strategy work have helped clients realize their product potential, resulting in billions of dollars of cost-savings and new revenue, and contributed to millions of happy customers.

At Intuit, I make QuickBooks Payments experiences more delightful for small businesses. Prior to Intuit, I designed consumer applications at Esurance and SpeedDate, and consulted for various clients including PayPal, Citrix and Skype.

Originally from Texas, I lived in New York City where I earned my BFA in Studio Art and Computer Science from NYU, and now reside in Mountain View, California.

Why I love what I do

Simplifying complex problems with elegant solutions

Few things are more satisfying to me than transforming something that was previously confusing or difficult into something that's intuitive and delightful.

Learning basic truths about human behavior and design principles

Understanding the science behind why we like or don't like things helps me be not only a better designer, but a better communicator, spouse and friend.

Gathering inspiration

I renew my creativity by reading, visiting physical and digital galleries, and playing with new apps and devices. Learning how others are solving challenges energizes me and I synthesize and apply their insights to my own work.

Collaborating with awesome people

Working with some of the best minds in business, engineering, and content strategy continually teaches me about other disciplines and instills in me a deep appreciation for their talents and craft. Together we develop better solutions than we would have each done separately.
